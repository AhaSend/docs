Replace AhaSend's domain with your own branded domain across all DNS records, providing a completely white-labeled email infrastructure for your customers.

<Tip>
**Platform Partner Feature:** DNS Whitelabel is available exclusively for Platform Partners (resellers). [Contact our sales team](https://ahasend.com/contact) to learn about our Platform Partner program.
</Tip>

## Overview

DNS Whitelabel allows Platform Partners to use their own domain for all email-related DNS records. Your customers will configure DNS records pointing to your domain instead of AhaSend, creating a seamless branded experience.
